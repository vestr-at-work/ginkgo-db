Enjoy some fun facts about Ginkgos

  • They are very old. Fossils very similar to the living species, belonging to the genus Ginkgo, extend back to the Middle Jurassic epoch approximately 170 million years ago.
  • Their branches look kinda weird, scientists would say erratic :)
  • They are queer legends and can convert sexes! Although Ginkgo trees are male or female, it has been observed that some branches can change sexes.
